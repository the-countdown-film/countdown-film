The Countdown

After endless nights and life-threatening fights with zombies, best friends Noah and Arden are on the verge of discovering The Hope, a sanctuary they’ve been chasing for days. But right when freedom is within reach, reality hits:Someone’s been bitten. With the clock ticking they must find a cure to hang on to their humanity and escape to freedom before it slips away forever.

  After countless nights battling and running from relentless zombies, Noah and Arden take one final pitstop in a laboratory for rations before escaping to a sanctuary known as The Hope. As the adrenaline from their bodies began to slow down, one began to feel an uncomfortable chill scatter across their bodies only to discover they had each been bitten. With the essence of time-consuming their humanity, Noah and Arden must scavenge through this deserted laboratory to find a cure before time runs out. With Arden eventually finding a hidden box of cures, she opens the box to discover that all cures have been ruptured except one. With a new challenge emerging Noah and Arden must face the sad reality of picking who’s life to liberate. After much debate, Arden finally convinces Noah to take the cure as a final thank-you gesture as she never knew much about family before she met him. As they hug for a final goodbye, Noah in a swift move injects the cure serum into Adren's back, restoring her right to live. Shocked and slightly upset by Noah’s actions, Arden is speechless, but before she speaks Noah reveals a secret to justify his actions. Forced to leave Noah behind, Arden leaves the laboratory alone to finish her journey to The Hope and a life of freedom.
